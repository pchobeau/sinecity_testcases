plot_att_spect module¶

plot_att_spect.attenuation_spectrums(f_fdtd, P_T_fdtd, P_F_fdtd, f_tlm, P_T_tlm, P_F_tlm, f_an, P_T_an, P_F_an, case)[source]¶

Plot the attenuation spectrums for case 3 in order to compare the numerical methods to the analytic solution. Six receiver positions are selected, which results in 6 subplots where the spectrums might be overlapped.

Parameters:
  • f_fdtd (1d list of floats) – frequency sequence for the fdtd (Hz).
  • P_T_fdtd (1d list [n] of floats) – total pressure in presence of BC for the fdtd (Pa).
  • P_F_fdtd (1d list [n] of floats) – free-field pressure for the fdtd (Pa).
  • f_tlm (1d list of floats) – frequency sequence for the tlm (Hz).
  • P_T_tlm (1d list [n] of floats) – total pressure in presence of BC for the tlm (Pa).
  • P_F_tlm (1d list [n] of floats) – free-field pressure for the tlm (Pa).
  • f_an (1d list of floats) – frequency sequence for the analytic solution (Hz).
  • P_T_an (1d list [n] of floats) – total field in presence of BC for the analytic sol. (Pa).
  • P_F_an (1d list [n] of floats) – free-field pressure for the analytic solution (Pa).
  • case (int) – integer that sorts of the saved folders in the results dir.
Returns:

the attenuation spectrums relative to free-field propagation.
