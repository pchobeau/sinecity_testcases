paumond_facade module¶

paumond_facade.main(d_sr, d_sr_ref, h_s, h_r, f_max_src)[source]¶

Each method (FDTD or TLM) is launched. The numerical error is calculated in level_calc_paumond_facade_2.py, and presented in boxplots.

Parameters:
  • d_sr (list of floats) – horizontal distances between the facade and the receivers (m).
  • h_s (float) – height of the source (m).
  • h_r (float) – height of the receiver (m).
  • f_max_src – approximated maximale frequency of the source signal -

Gaussian pulse - (Hz). :type f_max_src: float

Parameters:
  • case – integer that sorts of the saved folders in the results dir.
  • c – sound speed, float (m.s-1).
  • rho – air density, float (kg.m-3).
  • T – simulation duration, float (s).
  • h_set – spatial step sequence, list of floats (m).
  • dt_set – time step sequence, list of floats (s).
  • disp_inst_p – display the instantaneous pressure, boolean.
