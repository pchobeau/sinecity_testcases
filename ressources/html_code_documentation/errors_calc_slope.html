errors_calc_slope module¶

errors_calc_slope.error_calc(h_set, h_s, alpha, case, method_name)[source]¶
Parameters:
  • h_set (list of floats) – spatial step sequence (m).
  • h_s (float) – source heights (m).
  • alpha (float) – angle of the upward-sloping part (rad).
  • case (int) – integer that sorts of the saved folders in the results directory.
  • method_name (str) – name of the numerical method of interest.
  • res_path – path where to fetch the results for each method, string chain.
  • p_t_fdtd – total pressure in presence of the circular obstacle, 3d list [angle,distance,time].
  • p_f_fdtd – free-field pressure for the fdtd, 3d list [angle,distance,time].
  • f_fdtd – frequency sequence for the fdtd, 1d list of floats (Hz).
  • t_fdtd – time sequence for the fdtd, 1d list of floats (s).
  • Ts_fdtd – time step in the fdtd, float (s).
  • P_fdtd – Fourier transform of the scattered pressure for the FDTD, 3d list [angle,distance,frequency].
  • p_t_tlm – total pressure in presence of the circular obstacle, 3d list [angle,distance,time].
  • p_f_tlm – free-field pressure for the tlm, 3d list [angle,distance,time].
  • f_tlm – frequency sequence for the tlm, 1d list.
  • t_tlm – time sequence for the tlm, 1d list of floats (s).
  • Ts_tlm – time step in the tlm, float (s).
  • P_tlm – Fourier transform of the scattered pressure for the TLM, 3d list [angle,distance,frequency].
  • freq_idx – chosen frequency index within the range(max_freq_idx), scalar.
Returns:

The log-log plot of the two-norm and max-norm, and the respective orders of accuracy for the chosen frequency.
