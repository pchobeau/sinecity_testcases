case1_geospr module¶

case1_geospr.main(d_sr, f_max_src)[source]¶

Each method (FDTD or TLM) is launched above a ground, then in free field. The numerical error is calculated in error_calc_geospr.py

Parameters:
  • d_sr (list of floats) – horizontal distances between the source and the receivers (m).
  • f_max_src (float) – approximated maximale frequency of the source signal (Hz).
