plot_pres_slope_norot module¶

plot_pres_slope_norot.plot_pres_slope_without_rotation(res03, res13, alpha, h_s, freq, dx, n_idx_step, num_meth)[source]¶

Two figures that gives the absolute pressure obtained from the analytic solution (Rasmussen).

Parameters:
  • res03 (array of floats) – absolute value of the total pressure: incident+diffracted for the flat part (Pa).
  • res13 (array of floats) – absolute value of the total pressure: incident+diffracted after corner (Pa).
  • alpha (float) – angle of the upward-sloping part (rad).
  • h_s (float) – source heights (m).
  • freq (float) – frequency of the source signal (Hz).
  • dx (float) – spatial step (m).
  • n_idx_step (int) – number of indexes skipped during the FFT calculation
  • num_meth (str) – name of the numerical method
  • z_min – minimal magnitude for the scale of the colorbar, float.
  • z_max – maximal magnitude for the scale of the colorbar, float.
Returns:

2D plot of the acoustic pressure for a given frequency
