level_calc_paumond_facade module¶

level_calc_paumond_facade.levels(d_sr, d_sr_ref, case, method)[source]¶

Calculation of the levels for the receivers defined on the segment d_sr.

Parameters:
  • d_sr (list of floats) – horizontal distances between the facade and the receivers (m).
  • d_sr_ref (list of floats) – horizontal distances between the facade and the pedestrian (m).
  • case (int) – integer that sorts of the saved folders in the results dir.
  • method (string) – name of the numerical method used for the pressure calculation.
Returns:

the levels in dB SPL
Return type:

list of floats (maybe specrums ?)
